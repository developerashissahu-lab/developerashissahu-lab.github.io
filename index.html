Age calculator

About this tool

Calculates your exact age based on your birth date. It also shows how many days until your next birthday. Age is calculated by comparing today's date with your birth date, accounting for months and days.

Loan calculator

About this tool

Calculates monthly payments and total interest for a fixed-rate loan using the standard amortization formula. Results show your monthly payment and the total interest you'll pay over the loan term.

Love calculator

About this tool

A fun compatibility score based on the letters in your names. Each letter has a numeric value, and the combined total generates a percentage between 50-100%. Just for entertainment!
